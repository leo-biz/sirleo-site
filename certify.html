What You Learn

The Full Curriculum

This program is for men who are serious about building a professional practice. Hobbyists need not apply. If you want to offer sessions, build a client base, and do it ethically — this is the path.

01
Technique & Safety
Impact, restraint, sensation, edge play. How to execute cleanly. What can go wrong, how to prevent it, and how to handle it when it does.
02
Consent & Ethics
SSC, RACK, and modern frameworks for professional practice. Intake, negotiation, safe words, and aftercare as non-negotiables — not suggestions.
03
Client Management
Screening, communication, setting expectations, and maintaining professional distance. How to have clients without letting them run you.
04
Business Setup
Pricing strategy, how to build a booking system, marketing without oversharing, and the infrastructure that makes a practice sustainable.
05
Supervised Sessions
Real practice with real people. Supervised and debriefed — you get hands-on reps before you're working alone with clients.
06
Certification
Upon completion, you're listed as a certified provider within the Sir Leo network — with access to referrals, continued mentorship, and community.
