Questions

Before you
reach out.

The details that matter most: discretion, safety, pricing, and what happens after you inquire.

Is this confidential?

Yes. Your name, contact information, interests, and session details are handled discreetly. Nothing is shared publicly or casually.

I have no experience. Is that okay?

Yes. Many clients are new or quietly curious. Intake exists to understand your limits, language, pace, and desired intensity before anything is confirmed.

What happens after I submit an inquiry?

Sir Leo reviews it personally and follows up. If there is a fit, the next step is a conversation about goals, boundaries, logistics, and the right session structure.

What do private sessions cost?

Current 1-hour private session packages are $200, $300, and $400 depending on intensity. Add-ons and time extensions are confirmed during intake.

Are couples and group events available?

Yes. Private sessions can be designed for individuals or couples. Group experiences, bachelorette parties, birthdays, and private events are handled through the events inquiry flow.

How do safety and consent work?

Nothing begins without clear consent and mutual understanding. Limits, safe words, intensity, aftercare, and relevant health considerations are discussed before the session.

Where do sessions happen?

Sessions are arranged at a private, discreet Chicago location. Exact logistics are shared only after intake and confirmation.
